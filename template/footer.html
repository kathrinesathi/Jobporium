About

Our web-app enables the candidates looking for Jobs in their domains. It has a simple and convenient user interface so that Job seekers can register themselves with their resumes and they can look up different jobs according to their qualifications.It makes the potential candidates and employees get connected and get more knowledge. Job seekers can also update their skill sets whenever they want. Employees can also register and publish their jobs which enables them to find the right candidates for the vacant positions.
